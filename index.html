--- Prince of Paths - A being controlling the sanctioning of new paths
-|- Pathmaker - Reserved for the one who carved out the path
-*- Ruler - The current ruler of the path
-0- Aspect - The domain of the path
-'- Champion - An ascended in service of the path
-X- Opposer - An ascended in opposition of the path's ruler
-~- Army - Ascended beings in service of the path as a whole
-^- Priests - Non-ascended beings in service of the path as a whole
-=- Servant - A non-ascended in service of the path
